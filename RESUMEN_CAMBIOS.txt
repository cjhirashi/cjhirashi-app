PALETA DE COLORES APLICADA

BACKGROUNDS:
  • Primary: bg-slate-900
  • Gradient Layout: from-slate-900 via-cyan-900 to-slate-900
  • Cards: bg-slate-900/50
  • Inputs: bg-slate-800/50
  • Navbar: bg-slate-900/30

BORDERS & TEXT:
  • Card borders: border-cyan-500/20
  • Input borders: border-cyan-500/30
  • Primary text: text-cyan-100
  • Secondary text: text-cyan-300/60 a /80
  • Accent text: text-cyan-400, text-cyan-300
  • Links: text-cyan-400 (hover: text-cyan-300)

GRADIENTS:
  • Títulos: from-cyan-400 to-blue-500
  • Botones: from-cyan-500 to-blue-500 (hover: darker)
  • Éxito: from-green-400 to-cyan-400
  • Error: from-red-400 to-orange-400
  • Separadores: cyan-500/20 gradients

EFECTOS:
  • Glassmorphism: backdrop-blur-xl
  • Sombras: shadow-2xl
  • Border-radius: rounded-2xl (cards), rounded-lg (inputs)
  • Transiciones: transition-all duration-200
  • Animaciones: blobs, spinners
